Fire safety Better safe than sorry

New regulations for HMO properties mean costly monthly fire safety checks. Emergency lighting, smoke detectors, escape routes - it all needs to be checked and documented. Every month. Severe penalties exist for non-compliance.

With FireRadar you can easily create a monthly report to stay on the right side of the law. It's so easy that the tenant can do it for you with the app. This saves you from paying someone else to do it, and saves tenants from the intrusion.

  • Evidence of ComplianceYour safety proedures will be scrutinised after a fire. FireRadar enables you to proove that you've been fully compliant with the law.
  • Secure Cloud StorageAll your fire safety inspection reports are stored securely in the cloud as a backup. You'll always have a complete set of records going back years.
  • Tenant OperatedYou don't have to do the fire checks yourself, and you don't have to pay someone else to do it. Tenants can do it themselves and save the intrusion.

How it works Easy as 1, 2, 3

  2. Setup Configure your properties on the companion website. Specify exactly what needs checking with the help of our expert knowledge.
  3. Snap Snap photos, make notes, and answer compliance questions. The app creates reports in real-time as tenants survey the property.
